Who we are

About Connecting Dots

We are execution consultants who've been in the trenches of early-stage startups and know what it takes to ship fast without burning out your team.

Our philosophy

Connecting Dots is a consulting company that helps early stage startups ship fast and collaborate effectively so they get more tries at being wrong before they eventually get it right.

We take a no-nonsense approach — delivered with a soft touch — to putting in place simple processes and smooth collaboration techniques. No boilerplate. No buzzwords. Just practical tools that stick.

We believe that with the right foundations in place, any startup team can become both robust and fast — and that's the combination that gets you to product market fit.

🧩

What we do differently

Most consultants give you a report and disappear. We stay with you until the changes actually stick. Whether that's through our Guidance Program or by embedding directly with your team in the Launchpad, we're in it with you.

We have a particular eye for the human side of execution — identifying hidden gems in your team, unlocking collaboration, and making sure improvements outlast our engagement.
