Our foundational engagement. Diagnostic, prescription, plan — so you know exactly what to fix and in what order.

What it is

The Shipping Accelerator is where every engagement starts. We do a thorough benchmark of the current state of your organisation — your tech, your processes, and your people — and identify what solutions make the most sense in your specific case.

You get a clear picture of where you are, a prioritised prescription of what to change, and a concrete plan to make it happen. No generic frameworks. No copy-paste methodology.

🔬

What you walk away with

A clear, actionable plan you can start executing the week after we're done. Most teams feel the difference within the first sprint.

The Shipping Accelerator also unlocks access to our two follow-up services — the Guidance Program and the Launchpad — each designed to help you put the recommendations into practice with different levels of involvement from our side.
