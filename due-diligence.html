Deep Dive Due Diligence

A comprehensive technical due diligence engagement for post-term-sheet, when the stakes are high and you need the full picture.

Enquire now

No stone unturned

Once a term sheet is signed, the pressure to close is real — but so is the risk of missing something important. The Deep Dive Due Diligence gives you a complete technical picture before you wire the money.

We go deep on the codebase, the architecture, the team's practices, the technical debt, the security posture, and the scalability of what's been built. You get a comprehensive report that either confirms your conviction or surfaces the issues you need to negotiate on.

🔍

Built for investors, useful for portfolio teams too

Many of our VC clients also share the Deep Dive report with the portfolio company after investment closes — it becomes the starting point for a Shipping Accelerator engagement, turning due diligence into an immediate value-add for the startup.
